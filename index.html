Welcome to my web page, I'm Riccardo Andreoni.

I graduated in Mechatronics Engineering while growing a passion for optimization algorithms. My enthusiasm for data and optimization led me to perform research activities and publish peer-reviewed articles in a high-impact journal about genetic algorithms for solving Multi-Objective Optimization problems.

I have spent the past years immersing myself in data and machine learning. I attended several courses held by top institutions, and I've been publishing articles about AI and deep learning on TowardsDataScience.

From the beginning of my academic and working career, I proved to be capable of easily learning new software and concepts, then implementing them. This website, which I built by myself (starting from a template) is a demonstration of that.
