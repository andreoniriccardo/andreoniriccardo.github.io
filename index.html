Welcome to my web page, I'm Riccardo Andreoni.

I hold a strong academic background in numerical fields, with a full-mark Master's degree in Mechatronics Engineering and a solid foundation in statistics. My passion for Data and Optimization Algorithms led me to perform research activities and publish peer-reviewed articles in a high-impact journal about genetic algorithms for solving Multi-Objective Optimization problems.

In my professional career, I have worked as a production Data Analyst and currently hold a position as a Data Scientist, both in multinational companies. I have spent the past years immersing myself in Machine Learning model development and database management. One of my notable strengths lies in my communication skills. I am a regular contributor to Towards Data Science, a top data science blog, where I communicate technical concepts effectively to diverse audiences.

From the beginning of my academic and working career, I proved to be capable of easily learning new software and concepts, then implementing them. This website, which I built by myself (starting from a template) is a demonstration of that.
